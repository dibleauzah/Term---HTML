Higher-Ed Exchange

Your Path to a World-Class Education in the USA

Welcome! Please Note: this website’s sister site, for applied organizational and strategic communication research and consulting can be found via this link: www.profseifsekalala.org/consulting

Thanks for visiting our site.

The Higher-Ed Exchange consulting agency is your premier guide through the myriad priceless higher education opportunities of the USA. East African middle class families who know the value of a world-class education have long sought education in the UK and Europe, South Africa, India, among other nations.

But for a number of reasons, the US higher education system has mystified and discouraged numerous families from pursuing their dream of a quality university education.

That’s where we come in. Via this site, meetings with the HigherEdExchange East African regional director, and correspondence with the US-based chief consultant, parents and students will be taken step-by-step through the process of researching, applying to, and succeeding in American colleges* and universities.

—————————————

*In the USA, the word “college” denotes a university. Many colleges are of a smaller size compared to bigger universities, but their academic ranking is often the same, or higher. These institutions also often confer advanced (Masters and Doctorate) degrees.
